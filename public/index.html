August 20-27, 2017.

This year, McLaren will reunite with returning rally fans and welcome new ones as we motor north to Monterey from Los Angeles to celebrate Car Week once again. As we have in the past, we’ll spend three days traveling toward Pebble Beach, touring the coast in pursuit of culinary adventures, scenic driving and a few adrenaline-filled surprises. Joining us on the rally this year are a few very special guests that are sure to make every moment memorable and unique.

For those of you who are new to the rally, we hope this is the beginning of a thrilling annual tradition. And for those who are returning — can you feel the excitement building?

We’ve planned an amazing week and can’t wait for you to join us.
