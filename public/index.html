Pebble Beach: August 20-27, 2017.

This year, McLaren will reunite with returning rally fans and welcome new ones as we motor north to Monterey from Los Angeles to celebrate Car Week once again. As we have in the past, we’ll spend three days traveling toward Pebble Beach, touring the coast in pursuit of culinary adventures, scenic driving and a few adrenaline-filled surprises. Joining us on the rally this year are a few very special guests that are sure to make every moment memorable and unique.

For those of you who are new to the rally, we hope this is the beginning of a thrilling annual tradition. And for those who are returning — can you feel the excitement building?

We’ve planned an amazing week and can’t wait for you to join us.

THE RITZ- CARLTON


Marina Del Rey: Monday, August 20

Arrive at your leisure into Los Angeles International Airport where our McLaren Hosts will transport you to the Ritz-Carlton, Marina Del Rey to freshen up before departing in your awaiting car for the scenic trip up the coast to Santa Barbara.

BELMOND EL ENCANTO


Marina Del Rey: Monday, August 20

Check in at the amazing Belmond El Encanto and unwind before joining your fellow McLaren rally guests for cocktails and dinner on the Catalina Terrance before a stunning Santa Barbara sunset.


Santa Ynez Valley: Tuesday, August 21

We’ll start the day with an energizing morning breakfast and rally briefing. Adventure awaits, however some of the details are being kept under wraps as the finishing touches are being made. What we can say is that your McLarens will be put through their paces as we have prepared a special autocross course for you to challenge fellow rally guests. A classic Santa Maria BBQ will be served afterwards with an extra helping of bragging rights. As we return to Santa Barbara, the afternoon has been left to you. Stop by a featured winery in the Santa Ynez valley. Streatch out by El Encanto’s pool overlooking the Pacific Ocean or peek into the shops along Santa Barbara’s famous State Street. Tales of the day’s activities are sure to fill the air over dinner under the stars at El Encanto’s Lilly Pond. And rumor has it, trophies will be handed out this evening.


The Road to Monterey: Wednesday, August 22

Arise to breakfast and a rally briefing as we say farewell to Santa Barbara before embarking North toward Monterey. Our group departure from the El Encanto makes for a great photo and the drive North along the coast will provide countless opportunities to capture the spectacle of McLarens driving toward Car Week. We’ll use three words to describe our lunch stop today: Spectacular, Unique, Delicious. Enough said.


The Road to Monterey: Wednesday, August 22

The late afternoon greets us as we arrive at the majestic and stylish Bernardus Lodge & Spa in Carmel Valley. A glass of wine greets you as our McLaren Hosts help you settle in for a memorable next few days of Car Week. Join us for an evening arrival party in the Bernardus Rose Garden, hosted by Mike Flewitt, to enjoy casual farm-to-table cuisine and a spectacular Carmel Valley sunset.

BERNARDUS LODGE & SPA


Carmel Valley: Thursday, August 23

Rise and shine to the first day of Car Week. Join us at McLaren’s exclusive Rally Guest parking area for Cars & Coffee. Gather with fellow Rally Guests while enjoying bespoke espresso, pastires and this year’s McLaren Experience at Bernardus Lodge & Spa activation for Car Week. Depart Bernardus for central coast’s 11-turn, 2.238-mile jewel of a road course, Laguna Seca. Enjoy lunch and views of the circuit from the McLaren hospitality suite before taking your car for a McLaren-exclusive parade lap around the track – an experience you cannot miss! Today ends with spectacular views of Carmel Valley perched on the terrace of the McLaren F1 estate. Enjoy an amazing dinner hosted by Zac Brown, and team, as they share memorable events from the year and provide insights into the McLaren motorsports future.


Bernardus Lodge & Spa: Friday, August 24

Breakfast provided on the Lucia Patio of the Bernardus Lodge & Spa. Enjoy rare collections of fine automobiles and motorcycles on the rolling greens of Quail Lodge & Golf Club at The Quail, A Motorsports Gathering. Head to the lawn at Bernardus Lodge & Spa and form a team to compete in McLaren’s 2nd Annual Bocce Tournament. Join us for a Cocktail Reception at the McLaren Experience at Bernardus Lodge & Spa. Enjoy dinner at your leisure this evening. Still hungry? Be ready for a late night In-N-Out flash mob.


Bernardus Lodge & Spa: Saturday, August 25

Start your day with breakfast on the Lucia Patio of the Bernardus Lodge & Spa. Jump on a private transfer to Laguna Seca to witness the Rolex Monterey Motorsports Reunion. McLaren hospitality all day. Enjoy dinner at your leisure this evening.


Bernardus Lodge & Spa: Sunday, August 26

Transportation and tickets to the Concours d’Elegance will make this day the highlight of the week. For early risers, a special McLaren Dawn Patrol mission will take place. Sip coffee and be the very first to view the cars of the Pebble Beach Concours d’Elegance. Enjoy hospitality as only McLaren can offer at our Brand Center on the famed 18th fairway of Pebble Beach Golf Links. Join us for Farewell Toast at Bernardus Lodge Lucia Patio.


Bernardus Lodge & Spa: Monday, August 27

Breakfast, farewells and departures
